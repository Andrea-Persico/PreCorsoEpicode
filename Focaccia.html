Focaccai with Caramelized Onion, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with weet caramelized onion, tart
craberries ,and creamy bride.It will become you new favorite hollidai starter!

Fresh cranbery

Ingredient

For the focaccia
  • 4cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoons oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces bride, cut into cubes( scape rind off with a paring nife if desired,thought it's not
    necessary)
